Fries

Description

Easy fast french fries made from scratch! These are the best fries you will ever have.

Ingredients and Tools

  • Potatoes - 1 per person is more than enough!
  • 1qt/ltr cooking oil - peanut oil is best
  • Seasonings - I just used salt, but this is where you can get creative!
  • Knife for cutting
  • Colander for rinsing
  • Pot for frying

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into 1/4" fry shapes. Or fatter if you like "steak fries." I like em skinny and crisp!
  2. Rinse cut potatoes in a large bowl with lots of cold running water and cover with water by and ice. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
  3. In a large pan (or an electric deep fryer), heat oil over medium-low heat.
  4. If you have a candy thermometer (recommended), heat until the thermometer registers 325oF (165 C).
  5. Fry, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are soft and limp, about 10 minutes.
  6. Using a slotted spoon or sieve, carefully remove fries from the oil and set aside to drain on paper towels.
